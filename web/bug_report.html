How to ReportAegis Bugs

In order to not loose track of bug reports and feature requests, submit your requests using the approriate SourgeForge Tracker. They are available at http://sourceforge.net/tracker/?group_id=224. Please take the time to check whether your issue has already been reported before raising a new issue.

The trackers have been configured to send email notifications to the aegis-developers mailing list, where someone will investigate your issue.
