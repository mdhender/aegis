Other Free (Libre) Distributed VC and SCM Systems

.br There seems to be a fair bit of work going on in the field of free (libre) version control and software configuration management systems these days.
Here is an incomplete list of others who are having a go at similar systems. If you're unhappy with Aegis, maybe these will meet your needs better:
  • Codeville
  • Darcs
  • DCVS
  • GNU Arch
  • META-CVS
  • Monotone
  • OpenCM
  • PRCS
  • Stellation
  • Subversion
  • Vesta
Here is an incomplete list of comparisons of similar systems. If you're unhappy with Aegis, maybe these will help you find one that meets your needs better:
  • wiki.gnuarch.org
  • better-scm.berlios.de
If you know of others, please let me know and I'll add them here.
Maybe it's time for a “VC/SCM” web ring?
